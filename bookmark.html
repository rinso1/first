chapter 1

An academic essay is a focused piece
of writing that develops an idea or argument using evidence, analysis, and interpretation.

There are many types of essays you might write as a student. The content
and length of an essay depends on your level, subject of study, and course
requirements. However, most essays at universi ty level are argumentative—they aim to persuade the
reader of a particular position or perspective on a topic.
